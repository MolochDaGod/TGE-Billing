## 🔧 Step 3: Set Up Google Business Profile API (For Automation)

### **A. Enable the API (FREE)**

1. Go to: **https://console.cloud.google.com/**
2. Sign in with **tgebilling@gmail.com**
3. Create a new project: **"T.G.E. Billing - Business Profile"**
4. Enable APIs:
   - **Google Business Profile API**
   - **Google My Business API** (legacy - still needed)
5. Create OAuth 2.0 credentials:
   - Application type: **Web application**
   - Authorized redirect URIs: 
     - `https://tgebilling.pro/api/auth/google-business/callback`
     - `https://t-g-e.replit.app/api/auth/google-business/callback`
6. Download credentials JSON
7. Save **Client ID** and **Client Secret** in Replit Secrets

### **B. OAuth Scopes Needed:**
```
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/business.manage
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/plus.business.manage
```

These allow you to:
- Create posts
- Respond to reviews
- Update business info
- Read insights

## 🎯 Best Practices for Local SEO

### **Post Frequency:**
- **Minimum:** 2-3 times per week
- **Optimal:** Daily posts
- **Best days:** Thursday-Sunday (peak search times)

### **Content Mix:**
- 40% Completed jobs (before/after)
- 30% Educational (safety tips, electrical facts)
- 20% Promotional (special offers, seasonal)
- 10% Behind-the-scenes (team, process, equipment)

### **Photo Requirements:**
- **Minimum size:** 720px x 720px
- **Recommended:** 1200px x 1200px
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Avoid:** Text overlay (Google prefers clean images)

### **Response Time:**
- **Reviews:** Respond within 24 hours (Google ranks you higher!)
- **Messages:** Respond within 1 hour during business hours
- **Questions:** Answer same day
