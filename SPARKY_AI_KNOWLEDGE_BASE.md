### Component Resources
**DataSheets.com (https://www.datasheets.com/category)**
- Circuit Breakers (all major manufacturers)
- Transformers (step-up, step-down, isolation)
- Switches (toggle, dimmer, smart, specialty)
- Relays and Contactors
- Motor Controls and Starters
- Panel Boards and Load Centers
- Technical Documentation and Wiring Diagrams

**How Electricians Use It:**
- Search manufacturer part numbers before ordering
- Verify voltage/amperage ratings on-site
- Find installation guides and wiring diagrams
- Check compatibility before replacements
- Access technical specs for quotes

### Texas Wholesaler APIs (Live Pricing)
**1. Grainger API**
- Industrial electrical supplies
- Real-time pricing (requires contractor account)
- Stock availability lookup
- Bulk pricing for large commercial jobs

**2. Rexel/Gexpro API**
- Commercial electrical wholesale
- Contractor pricing access
- Inventory management integration
- Regional distribution centers

**3. CED (City Electric Distributors)**
- Local Texas locations
- Real-time pricing for quotes
- Will-call and delivery options
- Contractor account benefits

**4. WESCO API**
- Electrical, industrial, communications MRO
- API integration for automated quotes
- Multi-location pricing
- Volume discount structures

**Best Practices for Wholesalers:**
- Sign up for contractor accounts with 3-5 local wholesalers
- Compare pricing via API for jobs over $500 in materials
- Build relationships with reps for expedited service
- Use APIs to generate accurate quotes with current wholesale costs + markup
- Track seasonal pricing trends for bulk purchases (wire, panels, conduit)

### Customer Management Best Practices

**Incoming Lead Management:**
- **Immediate Response:** 24/7 booking system (speed wins jobs)
- **Qualification Questions:** Emergency? Residential/Commercial? Budget? Timeline?
- **SMS Confirmation:** Instant booking confirmation with tech details
- **Pre-Service Communication:** Safety protocols, parking, what to expect

**During Service:**
- **Photo Documentation:** Before/during/after for transparency
- **Scope Changes:** Communicate additional needs discovered
- **Transparent Pricing:** Explain costs before proceeding
- **Client Education:** Explain what was done and why

**Post-Service Follow-Up:**
- **Thank You SMS:** Within 24 hours of job completion
- **Review Request:** Best time is right after successful job
- **Referral Incentive:** Offer referral reward immediately
- **Seasonal Touchpoints:** Maintenance reminders, safety checkups

**Review Generation System:**
- Automated requests after completed jobs
- Timing is critical (send within 24 hours while experience is fresh)
- Multiple platform focus (Google, Facebook, Yelp, Nextdoor)
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative) professionally
- Showcase reviews on website and social media
